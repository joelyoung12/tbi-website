    Geographic Information Systems

    The range of company’s GIS activities cover fields of high technology and GIS applications’ development in particular (desktop / intranet / internet), database development, collection of digital data, digital process of satellite images, as well as electronic equipment (hardware) and field’s work instruments purchase, support, education and consulting services. We specialize in Mapping and we provide state-of-the-art solutions related to Mapping, Spatial Data Analysis, Geo-processing, Spatial Data Management, Network Analysis Services, Surveying and Urban & Regional Planning. Apart from our standardized maps, we also provide tailor made maps according to their specification and needs of our clients. Furthermore, we can help our clients visualizing technical information in a way which is easy to comprehend and assists in decision making. TBI Limited offers one stop solution to all our clients mapping need.
